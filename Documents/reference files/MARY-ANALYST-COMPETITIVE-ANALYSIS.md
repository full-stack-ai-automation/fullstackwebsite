## Glossary of Terms

**B2B:** Business-to-Business (selling to companies, not consumers)

**Conversion Rate:** Percentage of website visitors who take desired action (fill out form, book call)

**CTA:** Call-to-Action (button or link asking visitors to do something)

**Lead Magnet:** Free resource offered in exchange for email address

**ROI:** Return on Investment (how much money you make from an investment)

**SEO:** Search Engine Optimization (ranking higher in Google searches)

**Social Proof:** Evidence that other people trust you (logos, testimonials, reviews)

**Schema Markup:** Code that helps Google understand your content better (improves SEO)

**Progressive Profiling:** Gradually collecting information about leads over multiple interactions

**Lead Nurture:** Series of emails/content that educates prospects over time

**Freemium:** Free version with paid upgrades

**Above the Fold:** Content visible without scrolling

**Bounce Rate:** Percentage of visitors who leave without interacting

**Exit Intent:** Technology that detects when someone is about to leave and shows them a popup
